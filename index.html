03 / Point of view

What I
believe.

Strong commercialization starts with a clear view of how enterprise AI is actually bought, deployed and expanded.

01

AI GTM is not conventional SaaS GTM.

The product, deployment model and buying criteria evolve during the sales cycle. Commercial teams must shape adoption, not just sell features.

02

A pilot without an expansion architecture is an expensive demo.

Proof criteria, ownership, procurement and rollout logic need to be engineered before the pilot begins.

03

Partnerships only work with explicit ownership.

Joint pipeline is created when incentives, account access, responsibilities and deal governance are concrete.

04

Trust is a buying condition.

Technical assurance, governance and risk determine whether enterprise AI adoption gets funded.
